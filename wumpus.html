Wumpus Game

Instructions

Welcome to the Wumpus Game!

Your goal is to find the gold and avoid the Wumpus.

You win this game if you hit the Wumpus or get the gold

Use the grid to move your player:

  • Click on a square to move to that position.
  • The Wumpus is always trying to get closer to you!
  • You can't move to the grey square because it's blocked by an obstacle

To shoot your arrow:

  • Click the arrow buttons to shoot in that direction.
  • If you hit the Wumpus, you win!
  • If you miss, you'll use up your arrow.

Good luck!
